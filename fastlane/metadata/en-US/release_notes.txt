What's new in 1.3.2

New
• Light mode: Settings > Appearance now offers System, Light, or Dark — the app was dark-only before
• Delivery receipts: a prompt shows "sending", then "delivered" once your computer has it, and a dropped connection resends it safely
• Failed turns no longer look blank — an API-failure turn shows as an error, and a session near its context limit warns you before your next message

Fixes & improvements
• AskUserQuestion cards advance to the next question automatically after you pick a single-choice answer
• Codex sessions list under their real title instead of the first line of your prompt
• Reliability fixes on the computer side: an optional isolated daemon login (so phone-driven work can't sign your terminal's Claude out mid-task) and crash-safe transcripts

Note: some fixes need the computer-side cc-pocket-daemon on 1.3.2 — run "cc-pocket-daemon update" (or brew upgrade / scoop update).
