What's new in 1.3.5

New and improved since 1.3.4
• Claude, Codex, and Cursor now share one consistent new-session mode screen, including risk confirmation
• Approvals show risk, impact scope, and authorization history; the activity timeline records decisions, tools, and completion
• Codex usage shows the local official limit snapshot, weekly allowance, and reset time
• Better handoff across devices with takeover guidance, return summaries, and restored reading position
• Six-digit pairing codes submit automatically and dismiss the keyboard; scanner recovery is clearer
• Chats reopen at the last reading position or latest message, and the keyboard no longer hides recent content
• Throttled incremental rendering makes long streaming replies smoother; offline and empty-state recovery is clearer
• Updated OpenAI Codex branding, a clean native iOS photo symbol, and letter-free project avatars

Compatibility: daemon-backed additions require daemon 1.3.5 or newer. An installed 1.3.18 daemon should not be downgraded.
