What's new in 1.3.4

Fixes & improvements
• Chinese input is solid: fast pinyin no longer stutters or falls back to English, punctuation stops eating characters, and Send clears the box reliably
• Long replies are no longer cut off behind the composer
• Task panel: running jobs show elapsed time, can be stopped from your phone with a long-press, and settle instantly
• Usage: the overview follows Today/7d/30d with a labeled comparison; trend bars share one baseline and the peak label stays visible
• Changes viewer: diffs and text are selectable, with a new soft-wrap toggle
• New sessions show their effective model up front; switching models mid-session now applies next turn instead of interrupting the reply

Note: some fixes need the computer-side cc-pocket-daemon on 1.3.4 — run "cc-pocket-daemon update" (or brew upgrade / scoop update).
